## 11. Success Metrics

Track these KPIs to measure feature success:

1. **Adoption Rate**: % of stores creating at least one deal
2. **Active Deals**: Average number of active deals per store
3. **Deal Performance**: Average redemption rate across all deals
4. **Revenue Impact**: % increase in store revenue with deals vs without
5. **Customer Engagement**: % of orders using deals

**Target Goals (6 months)**:
- 60% of stores create at least one deal
- 2-3 active deals per store on average
- 15-20% redemption rate
- 25% revenue increase for stores with deals
- 40% of orders include a deal
